About Futsal City

Our Mission

To develop complete athletes through futsal, promoting values like teamwork, discipline, and respect, while improving technical and tactical skills.

Our Vision

To be a reference in futsal training, recognized for excellence in athlete development and for our contribution to the sport in our community.

Our Achievements

More than 50 championships won, hundreds of athletes developed, and a passionate community that grows every day. Our legacy is built on dedication.

Our Programs

Specialized programs for every age group and skill level

Ages 5-8

Futsal Initiation

Introduction to futsal through fun and playful activities. Focus on motor skills, coordination, and a love for the sport.

  • Basic fundamentals
  • Educational games
  • Social development

Ages 9-14

Technical Development

Refinement of technical and tactical skills. Preparation for competitions and development of strategic thinking.

  • Advanced technique
  • Game tactics
  • Regional competitions

Ages 15+

High Performance

Intensive program for athletes seeking excellence. Physical, technical, and mental preparation for high-level competitions.

  • Intensive training
  • Physical conditioning
  • National competitions
